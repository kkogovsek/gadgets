# Privacy Policy

*Last updated: March 31, 2026*

## Overview

Gadgets is a collection of browser-based utility tools. Your privacy is important — this policy explains what data is handled and how.

## Data stays on your device

All processing happens entirely in your browser. No data you enter — files, account numbers, names, or any other input — is ever transmitted to a server or third party.

## Local storage

Some gadgets use your browser's `localStorage` to remember settings between sessions (for example, your IBAN or account owner name in the NLB → SEPA tool). This data is stored only on your device and is never shared or sent anywhere.

You can clear it at any time through your browser's developer tools or by clearing site data.

## No tracking

Gadgets does not use analytics, cookies, tracking pixels, or any third-party scripts that could collect information about you or your usage.

## Files

Any files you upload (e.g. CSV exports) are read directly in your browser using the [File API](https://developer.mozilla.org/en-US/docs/Web/API/File_API). They are not uploaded anywhere and exist only in memory for the duration of your session.

## Contact

If you have any questions, open an issue on [GitHub](https://github.com/kkogovsek/gadgets).
